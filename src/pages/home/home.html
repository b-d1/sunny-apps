Welcome to ProTan.

Top tan is a free app, that gives you reasonable predictions and recommendations based on real data, to help you stay safe and healthier.

Our Goal

Our goal is to predict the weather and the current and conditions in the near future about the given beach, and recommendations on how to protect your self. The UV Radiation, HABs and the clouds can make a serious impact on your stay at the beach.

App features

Our app works with real world and accurate data, to provide you with high quality information. Our service will provide you with consistent and real-time information about the location you will provide.
